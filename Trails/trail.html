Difficulty: Moderate

Length: 6.9 miles

Skyline to Sweeny Ridge is a beautiful short hiking trail. The ridge’s 1200 ft high summit, covered with coastal scrub and grassland, slopes down to the San Francisco Bay on one side and to the Pacific on the other side. I would recommend you to do this hiking early in the morning and will take anywhere from 2 to 3 hours to complete depending on how much time you will spend resting. The views from the summit is beautiful with the view of the San Francisco Bay area. It might get muddy with light rain but other than that it is absolutely perfect.
